About Kevin Greenhough

<-- Look! It's me!

01.

Are you looking for a photographer with sub-par eyesight and a slight tremor that is exacerbated by a coffee addiction? Probably not. Yet here we are!

I realize this is the part where I’m supposed to regale you with everything that makes me so unique and special as a photographer. But if we’re both being honest, you don’t care, and I’m just a dude named Kevin who takes pictures sometimes. Let’s save the getting to know me for a socially distanced pint or two!

The gist of it is that I’ve been doing this for long enough to know what I’m doing, but not long enough to turn into an arrogant prima donna. I’m a fine wine that’s been matured to the point where additional aging yields diminishing returns.

My flavour profile first hits you with my habitually early arrival, followed by some humour and enthusiasm, and finally some distinct notes of work handed in on time.

If you like the sound of that, and you like the look of my photographs, then here's my number
